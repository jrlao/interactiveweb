About Mabuhay

Mabuhay in Tagalog, a language spoken in the philippines, means"hello".

Mabuhay as well in tagalog means "the will to live" through hardships life brings.

As we explore the project, we see how people question what life means, how we

question it, and how we get lost in it and want to bring meaning back to life.

This project was made by Janna Marie "Dani" La O'
